About Christian

Graduate of Florida State University

Chris attended Florida State from August 2015 to May 2019. He graduated Magna Cum Laude with two degrees: one in a Media/Communication Studies, the other in Sport Management. During his time at FSU, Chris developed a deep love of sports and the unity the bring.

    He was involved in a various facets of sport journalism:
  • Sports radio co-host with WVFS Tallahassee
  • Sportscaster with Seminole Productions
  • Sport writer for a brief moment with Seminoles.com
